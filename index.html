About

I studied at the Royal Academy of Music and Trinity College of Music in London, after which I enjoyed a busy teaching and performing life in classical, jazz, pop/rock, musicals and theatre in London. I performed on the hit TV show Made in Chelsea, in Big Ben for members of parliament and worked closely with Jay Aston from Bucks Fizz (winner of Eurovision 1981) as arranger and keyboard player.
